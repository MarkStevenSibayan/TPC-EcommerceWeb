Our Story

Founded in 2015, TPC Surplus began as a small operation in a garage in Silicon Valley. Our founder, Alex Chen, a former hardware engineer at a major tech company, noticed that many perfectly good computer components were being discarded during upgrade cycles. Seeing an opportunity to reduce electronic waste and provide affordable hardware to tech enthusiasts, Alex started collecting, testing, and reselling these components.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a thriving business. Today, TPC Surplus operates from a 15,000 sq ft warehouse and has expanded beyond surplus components to include new, cutting-edge hardware from the industry's leading manufacturers.

Our mission remains the same: to provide high-quality computer components at competitive prices while offering exceptional customer service and technical expertise.
