Our Journey

From Garage to Global

Founded in 2015, TPC Surplus began as a small operation in a garage in Silicon Valley. Our founder, Alex Chen, a former hardware engineer at a major tech company, noticed that many perfectly good computer components were being discarded during upgrade cycles.

Seeing an opportunity to reduce electronic waste and provide affordable hardware to tech enthusiasts, Alex started collecting, testing, and reselling these components.
